EVo - Event Hourglass

Brief

EVo FUDA Event Club is an Event Organization belonging to the possession of FPT University Danang Campus. We have officially organized and hold many Events and Ceremonies in FPT, also in Danang City. The first and foremost purpose of EVo is becoming an independent organization as well as approaching to be more professional & qualified community which can play an essential role in our society. We are fully dedicated and hearted to form up our own community where people can smoothly collaborate & intriguingly sacrifice for other’s happiness. We've continuously achieved the title of the most excellent club during two semesters.
