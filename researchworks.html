iNMe - BROKERAGE PLATFORM FOR CONSULTING SERVICES IN FINANCIAL INVESTMENT EXCHANGES

iNMe is an online brokerage application for consulting services in financial investment exchanges with the orientation of being an E-commerce trading platform developed on website and mobile application to meet the demand for financial investment advice at everytime & everywhere; integrated support for affiliate marketing, payment, online communication, information security. All in all, iNMe acts as an intermediary to meet the needs of the Multi-sided Market with two groups of customers: Marketing and promotion services, trade promotion for Investment Consultants and brokerage services which provide market research information, opinion polls and online portals for Investors.

AN INTELLIGENT SYSTEM FOR AUTOMATICALLY ASSESSING STUDENTS TO SUPPORT SOCIAL CONSTRUCTIVISM TEACHING METHOD

Recently, EduNext was developed by FPT Education as a platform that supports social constructivism methods in teaching and learning. The goal of this project is to solve the problem of automatically evaluating the quality of answers according to each criterion by using the Natural Language Processing Model (NLP Model) and Knowledge Graph. This AI system uses the lecture questions, assessment criteria, and students' final responses as input. Following processing, the output will be an answer's accuracy in relation to each criterion. Overall, this project will save teachers time by assisting them to provide more thorough assessments and objective ratings for each student.
