IT Company

KMS TECHNOLOGY

“This is the first time I have seen a Company Tour that students ask a lot and ask as good as this time” – Mr. Nguyen Van Hieu – Director of KMS Technology in Da Nang commented about F students during the Company Tour on April 14/ The past 2021. In this company tour, we had the opportunity to visit the actual working environment at the company and participate in a conversation with the experienced members of KMS. Established in 2009, KMS Technology is a company specializing in providing software product development services, consulting and implementing technology solutions and start-ups & investments. After 12 years of operation, KMS has built a team of more than 1,000 employees in the US and Vietnam, released 3 software products on the world market. In addition, KMS was voted as the top 5 IT companies and the top 32 best places to work in Vietnam.

Hotel & Resort

FURAMA RESORT DANANG

On the morning of January 12, FPT University students had a visit and practical experience at Furama Resort Danang. On this day, F students were introduced to the resort overview, items, rooms and shared about their profession. This will be a stepping stone for students to equip themselves with suitable options in the upcoming OJT term. Owning 198 luxurious rooms designed in Vietnamese and French cultural styles, delicately blended with local ChamPa architecture, Furama Resort is famous as a resort and cuisine of Vietnam. Furama Resort Danang has been voted by the World Hotel Association (WORLDHOTEL) as one of the 10 most desirable hotels in the world, one of the best resorts in Vietnam and Asia. Previously, Furama Resort Da Nang was one of the 5-star tourism businesses that signed a MOU cooperation agreement with FPT University Da Nang.
