F-CAMP 2020

F-Camp is the annual program of the previous students to welcome new students. With the theme "FPT students repel COVID", F-camp 2020 was prepared early by the BTC, gathered more than 1000 people and exploded into a great war between 4 villages F, P, T and U. AMAZING NIGHT 2021

Da Nang Department of Culture and Sports organized a foreign art performance program "Hello New Year 2021 - Amazing Night" with the participation of Vietnamese and foreign artists living and performing art activities in the Danang City, Hue and Hoi An. This is a program in the series of activities to welcome the new year 2021 to contribute to the restoration of cultural activities, tourism and services after the COVID-19 epidemic. The EVo Club is fortunate to once again be able to participate in supporting the organization together with the program organizers.
