FEN - Fantastic Environment Club

Brief

FEN is currently an Environmental Organization operating under the control of FPT University City Danang. To be the first and foremost community club in FPTU, we can't help the desire to change people's lifestyle which engaged in the awareness of conciously protecting the environment. We have achieved in supporting and organizing many events and small projects related to Environmental Propaganda and Campaign which somehow contributed a small part to help the Environment in the City or at least, our School's Campus to be cleaner and greener. Currently, we haven't been able to impact on the big society yet, but we've succeeded in thoroughly reforming our FENers.
-Be greener, be FENers!-
